Introduced in 2012, the CX-5 is Mazda’s first model to fully embrace the "Kodo" design language and Skyactiv technology. It's a compact crossover SUV known for sharp handling and fuel efficiency.

  • Top Speed: Around 130–140 mph (210–225 km/h)
  • 0-60 mph: : 6.1–8.7 seconds
  • Engine: : 2.0L or 2.5L inline-4 (later models include turbocharged variants)
  • Horsepower: 155–256 hp
