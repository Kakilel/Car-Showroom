The Suzuki Alto is a city car introduced in 1979. It has gone through several generations, popular for its affordability and efficiency. It's especially favored in Asian markets.

  • Top Speed: Approx. 90–100 mph (145–160 km/h)
  • 0-60 mph: About 13–15 seconds
  • Engine:Typically equipped with a 0.8L or 1.0L 3-cylinder engine, depending on the generation.
  • Horsepower: Around 48–68 hp
