Ferrari F8 Tributo

The Ferrari F8 Tributo is a mid-engine sports car produced by the Italian automobile manufacturer Ferrari. It was unveiled at the 2019 Geneva Motor Show. The car is an update to the 488 with notable exterior and performance changes.

  • Top Speed: 211 mph
  • 0-60 mph: 2.9 seconds
  • Engine: 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8
  • Horsepower: 710 hp
