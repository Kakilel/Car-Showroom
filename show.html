About Us

Elite Autos KE is a premier car dealership located in Westlands, Nairobi, along General Mathenge Road, opposite Kwacha House. Established in early 2025 by Kakilel Chebarwett, the dealership has rapidly gained recognition for offering a wide selection of high-quality vehicles, particularly specializing in certified pre-owned Mercedes-Benz models. Each vehicle undergoes a rigorous inspection process to ensure it meets the highest standards, providing customers with reliable and luxurious options. With a commitment to excellence, Elite Autos KE delivers fully registered cars and offers nationwide delivery services, catering to the diverse needs of its clientele.


What we offer

Beyond sales, Elite Autos KE provides comprehensive services, including stress-free financing options, maintenance, and trade-in assistance. The dealership prides itself on its customer-centric approach, ensuring a seamless and satisfying experience for every client. Testimonials highlight the dealership's dedication to quality and customer satisfaction, with clients praising the ease of transactions and the exceptional condition of the vehicles. Under the leadership of Director Vincent, Elite Autos KE continues to set the standard for luxury car dealerships in Nairobi, embodying a blend of professionalism, integrity, and automotive excellence.
