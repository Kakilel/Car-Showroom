The McLaren P1 is a hybrid hypercar that was produced from 2013 to 2015. It remains a benchmark for hybrid performance, often compared with the LaFerrari and Porsche 918 Spyder.

  • Top Speed: 217 mph (350 km/h)
  • 0-60 mph: 2.6 seconds
  • Engine: 3.8L twin-turbo V8 + electric motor (hybrid)
  • Horsepower: 903 hp
