The Urus is Lamborghini’s first modern SUV, launched in 2018. It combines luxury, utility, and the brand’s signature high performance. It uses a twin-turbo V8, breaking away from Lamborghini’s tradition of naturally aspirated engines.

  • Top Speed: 190 mph (305 km/h)
  • 0-60 mph:3.5 seconds
  • Engine: 4.0L twin-turbocharged V8
  • Horsepower: 641 hp
